Red soup

  • Time:30 minutes
  • Serves: 3 people
Ingredients Quantity
onion 1 piece
courgette 1 piece
spice a pinch
tomato in its juis 1 cann (400 g)

Production process:

  1. Cut onion and grill with spice in pan
  2. Cut courgette
  3. Put grilled onions, cuted courgette and tomatoes to pot
  4. pour everything in a liter of water
  5. boil soup about 15 minutes and blend everything
  6. Pour the soup to the plate and Enjoy!

Chicken

  • Time:1 hour
  • Serves: 3 people
Ingredients Quantity
chicken brests 500 g
chicken spices one pinch
ham 4 pieces
cremy cheese 200 g

Production process:

  1. Rub the chicken with spices
  2. Put cheese inside chicken piece and wrap it to the roll
  3. Wrap this roll to ham
  4. put chicken to the oven
  5. bake it about 50 minutes in 200 C

Cake: Lasy man

  • Time:30 minutes
  • Serves: 3 people
Ingredients Quantity
biscuits 300 g
butter 200 g
condencate milk one can (about 200 g)
cacao 4 spoons

Production process:

  1. breake biscuits to small paces
  2. mel butter on slow fire
  3. Put condencate milk and cacao together with butter and make one mass
  4. put chokolate mass on biscuits, mix them
  5. put cake to the freezer for 4 hours
  6. Serve and Enjoy!
